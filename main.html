Architectural Wonders

The Eiffel Tower in Paris is one of the most iconic architectural structures in the world. Constructed in 1889, it showcases the innovative use of iron in the late 19th century and stands as a global symbol of French culture and engineering.
The Colosseum in Rome is an architectural marvel from the Roman Empire, famous for its amphitheater design. Built around 70-80 AD, it represents Roman engineering mastery and is still admired today.
